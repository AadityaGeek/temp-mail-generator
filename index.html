Understanding Temporary Email

What is Temporary E-mail?

In today's digital world, almost every online service requires an email address for registration. While essential, this often leads to unwanted spam and privacy concerns. Our temporary email service provides a simple solution - instant, disposable email addresses that protect your primary inbox.

Instant Generation

Create a new temporary email address with just one click - no registration required.

Easy Copy

Copy your temporary email address to clipboard instantly with our convenient copy button.

Auto Refresh

Inbox automatically refreshes every 5 seconds to show new messages instantly.

Quick Delete

Delete your current email and generate a new one whenever you want.

How to Use Our Service

  1. 1

    Generate Email

    Click the "Generate Temp Email" button to create your temporary email address.

  2. 2

    Copy Address

    Use the copy button to copy your temporary email address to clipboard.

  3. 3

    Check Messages

    Your inbox will automatically refresh to show new messages. Click any message to view its full content.

  4. 4

    Delete When Done

    Click the delete button to remove the current email and generate a new one if needed.

Common Use Cases

  • Sign up for free trials without risking spam
  • Test website registrations and notifications
  • Protect your privacy when using public services
  • Avoid marketing emails from online stores
  • Receive one-time verification codes

Important Note

This service is designed for temporary use only. Do not use temporary email addresses for important accounts, financial services, or long-term communications. Messages are not stored permanently and the email address may be deleted after a period of inactivity.
